Experience


My experience includes work with the public, students, and researchers. Through these positions, I have become familiar with the resources needed for a diverse group of individuals. Whether it’s finding a board book for a baby or a Cochrane Review for a researcher, I have learned about a wide range of subjects and know the best way to make them accessible. I keep up with trends in children and teen literature, and even if I might not know the more minute details of complex subjects such as medicine, I always know where to look in order to retrieve the information needed.
